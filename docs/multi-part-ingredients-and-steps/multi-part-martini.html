Matcha Martini with Multi-part Ingredients and Steps

The cocktail brings umami to happy hour.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups ice
  • MAKE THE MATCH SYRUP
    • 2 tablespoons matcha
    • 2.25 cups water
    • 1¾ cups sugar
  • MAKE THE MARTINI
    • 2 ounces vodka
    • 1/4 ounce fresh lime juice
    • 3/4 ounce matcha syrup
  • 1 slice lime for garnish

Instructions

  • MAKE THE MATCH SYRUP
    1. In a pot over medium-high heat, combine 2 tablespoons matcha and 2 cups water and let it steep, whisking to break up clumps. Once incorporated, remove and strain, reserving the matcha water.
    2. Put back in the pot over heat and combine with 2 cups sugar, mix until dissolved. Remove from heat and let it cool before using. Store for up to two weeks.
  • MAKE THE MARTINI
    1. Add vodka, lime juice, and match syrup to an ice-filled cocktail shaker and shake until well chilled.
    2. Double-strain into a chilled cocktail coupe glass.

Note:Be sure to buy a good-quality matcha for this recipe, particularly because it’s the main ingredient and flavor of the dish.
