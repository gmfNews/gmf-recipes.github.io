Instructions

  1. Add vodka, blueberry lemonade, agave syrup, and lime juice to a cocktail shaker.
  2. Fill the cocktail shaker with ice and shake until the outside is frosted, about 30 seconds.
  3. Sprinkle Tajin seasoning on a plate, rub the rim of your favorite glass with a lemon or lime wedge, and dip the glass in seasoning to coat the rim. Strain the cocktail into the glass.
  4. Add Tabasco.
