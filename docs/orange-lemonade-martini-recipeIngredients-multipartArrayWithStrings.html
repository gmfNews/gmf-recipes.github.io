Orange Lemonade Martini

This orange lemonade martini is sweet, tart, cool, and refreshingly delicious. The flavors say summer, but the color might make you think holidays.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup lemon infused vodka
  • JUICES (array of strings)
    • 1/4 cup orange lemonade
    • 1 teaspoon freshly squeezed lime juice
  • SWEETENERS (array of strings)
    • 1 tablespoon agave syrup or simple syrup
  • 1 wedge plus 1 slice orange or tangerine, divided, for garnish
  • 1 tablespoon chili lime seasoning, such as Tajin

Instructions

  1. Add vodka, orange juice, agave syrup, and lime juice to a cocktail shaker.
  2. Fill the cocktail shaker with ice and shake until the outside is frosted, about 30 seconds.
  3. Sprinkle Tajin seasoning on a plate, rub the rim of your favorite glass with a orange or tangerine wedge, and dip the glass in seasoning to coat the rim. Strain the cocktail into the glass.
