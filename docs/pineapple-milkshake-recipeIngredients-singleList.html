Pineapple Milkshake

A quick and refreshing treat that's easy to make in minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup freshly diced pineapple
  • 1 pint vanilla ice cream
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 pinch ground cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Place the diced pineapple, ice cream, milk, and cinnamon into a blender or food processor.
  2. Cover and blend until the mixture is smooth.
  3. Pour into a glass and serve immediately.

Note: Feel free to experiment with different fruit or milk substitutes for variety.
